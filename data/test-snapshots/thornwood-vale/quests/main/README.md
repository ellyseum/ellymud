# Main Story Quests

The primary storyline of Thornwood Vale, following the player's journey from newcomer to hero.

## Story Arc

1. **The Road to Thornwood** (Level 2-5)
   - Travel from Millbrook Village to Thornwood Town
   - Discover the growing threats to the region

2. **The Forest Menace** (Level 5-8)
   - Investigate bandit activity in the forest
   - Uncover a larger conspiracy

3. **The Goblin Threat** (Level 8-12)
   - Assault the goblin caves
   - Defeat the chieftain and his forces

4. **Darkness Rising** (Level 12-15)
   - Face the true evil in Darkhollow Marsh
   - Confront the source of corruption

## Progression

Main quests should be completed in order. Each quest unlocks the next chapter of the story.
